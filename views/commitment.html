Commitment

Jiko Power empowers rural Africans by providing access to electricity in sustainable and cost-effective ways. By utilizing currently ‘wasted energy’ from existing cook stations, we provide a convenient and cost-effective solutions. We are committed to providing energy to those who cannot afford it!
